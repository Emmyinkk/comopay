Consumers

ComoPay is a comprehensive platform designed to provide accessible and affordable consumer credit services and innovations to working Nigerians across both formal and informal sectors of the economy. Our services cater to a wide range of individuals, including civil servants, corporate employees, military and paramilitary personnel, pensioners, artisans, farmers, traders, and more. We strive to ensure that all segments of society have unrestricted access to the financial solutions they need to thrive irrespective of their location, gender or income.

Merchants

ComoPay's merchants are businesses or individuals who partner with ComoPay to offer their goods or services to customers through the ComoPay platform. These merchants may include retailers, wholesalers, service providers, and other businesses across various industries. By partnering with ComoPay, merchants can expand their customer base, increase sales, and provide more flexible payment options to their customers, such as buy now pay later schemes or cash advances.

Consumer Agreegrators

ComoPay's Consumer Aggregators are often entities such as employers, trade unions, and cooperative societies that collaborate with ComoPay. In this capacity, they serve as administrators, aggregators, and guarantors for their employees or members.Through this partnership, ComoPay and Consumer Aggregators work together to provide access to financial services and solutions for their respective constituencies, ensuring convenience, security, and peace of mind.
